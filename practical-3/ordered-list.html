Diwali Shopping

  1. Diwali Diyas: Traditional oil lamps for decoration.
  2. Candles: Scented or decorative candles for ambiance.
  3. Rangoli Colors: Colored powders for creating rangoli designs.
  4. Firecrackers: Assorted fireworks for celebration.
  5. Sweets: A variety of Indian sweets (e.g., ladoos, barfis).
  6. Snacks: Savory snacks like chakli, namkeen, and murukku.
  7. Decorative Items: Wall hangings, torans, and other festive decor.
  8. Gifts: Presents for family and friends (e.g., chocolates, gift hampers).
  9. New Clothes: Traditional attire or festive wear for celebrations.
  10. Pooja Items: Essential items for the Diwali Pooja (e.g. flowers, incense).
