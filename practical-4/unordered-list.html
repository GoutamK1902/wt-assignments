Grocery Shopping List

  • Fruits: Apples, bananas, oranges, and seasonal fruits.
  • Vegetables: Potatoes, tomatoes, onions, and leafy greens.
  • Dairy: Milk, yogurt, and cheese.
  • Grains: Rice, wheat flour, and pulses.
  • Snacks: Chips, cookies, and nuts.
  • Spices: Turmeric, cumin, and garam masala.
  • Oils: Cooking oil and ghee.
  • Condiments: Ketchup, mustard, and pickles.
  • Beverages: Tea, coffee, and fruit juices.
  • Frozen Items: Frozen vegetables and ice cream.
